## Task Set 1: 3-4 Skills

### Task: Unpack Bottled Drinks
- **Goal:** Move bottled drinks from the counter to the cabinet.
- **Objects:** Bottled drink (such as water bottle, wine, beer)
- **Fixtures:** Cabinet
- **Skills:** Open(cabinet), Pick_up(bottled_drink), Place(cabinet), Close(cabinet)
- **Reasoning:** After shopping, one common activity is storing drinks appropriately for later use.

### Task: Unpack Packaged Foods
- **Goal:** Move boxed and canned foods from the counter to the cabinet.
- **Objects:** Packaged food (such as boxed food, canned food)
- **Fixtures:** Cabinet
- **Skills:** Open(cabinet), Pick_up(packaged_food), Place(cabinet), Close(cabinet)
- **Reasoning:** Packaged goods often need to be organized in the cabinet and this reflects a pantry organization activity.

### Task: Storing Baking Ingredients
- **Goal:** Move bar soap and cereal from the counter to the cabinet.
- **Objects:** Cleaner (bar soap), Packaged food (cereal)
- **Fixtures:** Cabinet
- **Skills:** Open(cabinet), Pick_up(cereal), Place(cabinet), Close(cabinet)
- **Reasoning:** It is common to store items used less frequently, like baking supplies, in a specific part of the cabinet after groceries.

### Task: Arrange Citrus Fruit
- **Goal:** Move all citrus fruit from the counter to the cabinet.
- **Objects:** Fruit (such as lemon, lime, orange, tangerine)
- **Fixtures:** Cabinet
- **Skills:** Open(cabinet), Pick_up(fruit), Place(cabinet), Close(cabinet)
- **Reasoning:** Sorting and storing similar types of items together, like citrus fruits, is an everyday kitchen organization activity.

### Task: Sort Dairy Products
- **Goal:** Move dairy products from the counter to the cabinet.
- **Objects:** Dairy (such as cheese, milk, yogurt)
- **Fixtures:** Cabinet
- **Skills:** Open(cabinet), Pick_up(dairy), Place(cabinet), Close(cabinet)
- **Reasoning:** Stowing away dairy products in a specific location helps keep the kitchen organized and ready for meal prep.

## Task Set 3: 8-10 Skills

### Task: Assemble Beverage Station
- **Goal:** Create a designated area on the counter for drinks like coffee cups, tea kettle, and water bottles.
- **Objects:** Drink (coffee cup, water bottle), Receptacle (kettle)
- **Fixtures:** Cabinet, counter
- **Skills:** Open(cabinet), Pick_up(coffee_cup), Place(counter), Pick_up(water_bottle), Place(counter), Pick_up(kettle), Place(counter), Close(cabinet)
- **Reasoning:** Arranging a beverage station is a convenience setup for immediate and easy access to drinks.

### Task: Set Up Snack Station
- **Goal:** Arrange a variety of snack foods like chocolates and chips on a tray in the cabinet for easy access.
- **Objects:** Sweets (chocolate), Packaged food (chips), Receptacle (tray)
- **Fixtures:** Cabinet
- **Skills:** Open(cabinet), Pick_up(tray), Place(counter), Pick_up(chocolate), Place(tray), Pick_up(chips), Place(tray), Place(tray (now filled with snacks) back in cabinet), Close(cabinet)
- **Reasoning:** A snack station with sweet and savory options is often set up for family and guests to access easily.

### Task: Organize Vegetables
- **Goal:** Segregate different types of vegetables into separate cabinets or drawers for better food management.
- **Objects:** Vegetable (avocado, cucumber, potato)
- **Fixtures:** Cabinet, drawer
- **Skills:** Open(cabinet), Pick_up(avocado), Place(cabinet), Close(cabinet), Open(drawer), Pick_up(cucumber), Place(drawer), Close(drawer), Open(cabinet), Pick_up(potato), Place(cabinet), Close(cabinet)
- **Reasoning:** Organizing different types of vegetables into separate compartments helps in maintaining freshness and accessibility.

### Task: Assemble Cooking Station
- **Goal:** Arrange cooking essentials like pots and kettles on the stove for immediate use.
- **Objects:** Receptacle (pot, kettle)
- **Fixtures:** Stove
- **Skills:** Pick_up(pot), Place(stove), Pick_up(kettle), Place(stove), Twist_knob(stove) (to simulate preparing for cooking)
- **Reasoning:** Prepping the cooking area by placing cookware on the stove is a prelude to meal preparation.

### Task: Dinnerware Organization
- **Goal:** Arrange plates, bowls, and mugs in the cabinet post-shopping.
